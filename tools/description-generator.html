Don't leave your description blank. A structured description helps YouTube understand your video and rank it higher in search results.

Why YouTube Descriptions Matter for SEO

The first 200 characters of your description are crucial. They appear in search results and help viewers decide whether to click. Additionally, YouTube's algorithm scans your description for keywords to understand the context of your video.

Anatomy of a Perfect Description

  • The Hook: State clearly what the video is about in the first sentence using your main keyword.
  • Timestamps: Essential for long videos. They create "chapters" in the progress bar and appear in Google Search.
  • Links: Direct traffic to your other social platforms, products, or affiliate offers.
  • Hashtags: Add up to 3 hashtags at the bottom. These appear above your video title on mobile.
