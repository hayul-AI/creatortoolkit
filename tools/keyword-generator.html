How to Use Keywords on YouTube

Keywords help YouTube's algorithm understand what your video is about. While "tags" are less important than they used to be, keywords in your Title, Description, and Spoken Content (captions) are vital.

Strategy: The "Long-Tail" Approach

Instead of targeting broad terms like "Fitness" (which has millions of videos), target specific phrases like "10 minute fitness routine for beginners at home". These are called long-tail keywords. They have less competition and higher intent, making it easier for new channels to rank.
