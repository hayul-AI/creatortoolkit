SEO Strategy: The Long Tail

What are Long-Tail Keywords?
Instead of targeting broad terms like "Fitness" (millions of results), target specific phrases like "10 minute fitness routine for beginners" (fewer results, higher intent).

Why use them?
New channels cannot compete on broad terms. Long-tail keywords have less competition, making it easier for you to rank on Page 1 of search results.
