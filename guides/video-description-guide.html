Writing SEO-Optimized Video Descriptions

Published on February 27, 2026

The description is where you tell the algorithm and the viewer more about your video. It's a critical place for SEO and driving traffic to other links.

1. The "Above the Fold" Hook

The first two lines are visible without clicking "Show More." Include your main keyword and a compelling summary here to help with search results.

2. Use Timestamps

Timestamps (e.g., 02:30 - How it works) create "Chapters" in your video. This improves user experience and allows your video to appear in Google Search for specific queries.

3. Keywords and Hashtags

Include 3-5 relevant hashtags at the bottom. Use our Description Generator to instantly create optimized metadata including hashtags.

4. Calls to Action (CTA)

Always tell your viewers what to do next. Whether it's subscribing, checking a link, or leaving a comment, a clear CTA increases engagement.

Conclusion

Descriptions don't need to be essays. Focus on clarity, keywords, and providing value to the viewer who wants to learn more about your content.
