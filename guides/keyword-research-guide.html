Keyword Research for YouTube Growth

Published on February 27, 2026

Keyword research is the process of finding what people are actually searching for. By targeting the right keywords, you can bypass the "zero views" phase and get discovered immediately.

1. Target Long-Tail Keywords

Instead of broad terms like "Gaming," target specific phrases like "How to beat [Boss] in [Game]." These have lower competition and higher intent.

2. Analyze Search Intent

Are people looking for a tutorial, a review, or entertainment? Match your content style to the search intent of the keyword.

3. Use Our Keyword Tool

Our Keyword Generator analyzes seed keywords to suggest viral variants and long-tail terms optimized for 2026 trends.

4. Look at "Suggested" Keywords

Check the search bar on YouTube for auto-complete suggestions. These are the most common things people are typing right now.

Conclusion

SEO takes time, but it's the most sustainable way to grow. Combine your research with great packaging (title/thumbnail) for the best results.
