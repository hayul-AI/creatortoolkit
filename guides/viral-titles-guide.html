How to Write Viral YouTube Titles

Published on February 05, 2026

A great title can make the difference between 100 views and 100,000. While your thumbnail grabs attention, the title confirms the click.

The Curiosity Gap

The most powerful psychological trigger in titles is curiosity. This is the gap between what we know and what we want to know.

  • Bad: "I Bought a Car."
  • Good: "I Bought the Cheapest Car on Amazon."
  • Viral: "I Bought a $1 Car (It Actually Runs!)"

Negativity Bias

Humans are hardwired to pay more attention to negative information. Words like "Mistake," "Stop," "Don't," and "Warning" often outperform positive words. Use this responsibly—don't be alarmist, but highlight the stakes.

Specificity

Vague titles get ignored. Specific numbers and details build credibility and promise a tangible reward.

  • Vague: "How to Get More Views"
  • Specific: "Get 1,000 Views in 7 Days (New Strategy)"
